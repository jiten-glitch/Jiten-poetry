Alfaaz-e-Jiten...

Khamoshi bhi kabhi bahut kuch keh jaati hai,
Jo lafzon se na ho, wo dil se guzar jaati hai.
Har dard ko muskaan me chhupa liya humne,
Shayad isi ko zindagi kahi jaati hai.
— Jiten...
Waqt ne sikhaya hai sambhal kar chalne,
Log aksar apne hi nikalte hain badale.
Jo saath rahe andhere tak mere,
Unhi ke naam likhi hai maine ujale.
— Jiten...
Kuch sapne adhure hi achhe lagte hain,
Poori ho jaayein to kahani khatam ho jaati hai.
Dil isliye shayari karta rehta hai,
Taaki mehsoos karne ki wajah zinda rahe.
— Jiten...
